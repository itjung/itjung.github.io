I am a PhD student in the Department of Astronomy at The University of Texas at Austin, working with Dr. Steve Finkelstein. I work on observational astronomy about the distant universe, peering inside galaxies in the first two billion years after the Big Bang with the HST imaging, and searching for Lyman-alpha emitting galaxies at the epoch of reiniozation using deep ground-based spectroscopic observational data.
