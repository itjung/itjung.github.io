I am a NASA Earth and Space Science Graduate Fellow (NESSF), working with Prof. Steve Finkelstein in the Department of Astronomy at the University of Texas at Austin. I work on observational astronomy about the distant universe, peering inside galaxies in the first two billion years after the Big Bang with the CANDELS HST imaging, and searching for Lyman-alpha emitting galaxies as a probe of cosmic reiniozation using deep ground-based spectroscopic observational data (Keck+DEIMOS/MOSFIRE). [Read more about research...]

I am originally from South Korea. Before joining the UT Austin astronomy program, I obtained my Bachelor's (astronomy/physics) and Master's (astronomy) degrees at Yousei University in Seoul. [Read more about me...]
